\indent \indent Bit Rate = 2(22 KHz) * 10 b/p = 440,000 bps.\\

\indent \indent pulse over halfway from one level to another.\\

\indent \indent The system can support (1.1V ampl.)*2 / (.2V noise)*2 = 2.2/.4 = 5 levels of logic.\\
